## 1. Value Ladder Framework (Brunson)

### Canonical story (Brunson dentist)
Nha sỹ free teeth cleaning → Brunson đi vào → mua whitening ($297) → braces ($2,500) → cosmetic surgery ($15,000+). Mỗi tier giá tăng, value tăng. Bait free at the start = entire ladder revenue.

### Trục Value Ladder
- Trục Y = VALUE (giá trị trao cho khách)
- Trục X = PRICE (giá bán)
- Curve = ascending (Value tăng theo Price)

### 5-tier structure chuẩn

| Tier | Price range | Purpose | Volume |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Bait** (free / $7-97) | Free hoặc 199k-3M VND | Filter intent, qualify lead | 100% leads |
| **Frontend** ($97-497) | 1-5M VND | First paid, break-even OK | 20-30% leads |
| **Mid** ($497-1,999) | 5-30M VND | Profit zone, depth deliver | 5-15% leads |
| **High Ticket** ($5K-25K) | 30-300M VND | Premium offer, 1on1 | 1-5% leads |
| **Continuity** ($97-497/m) | 1-5M/tháng | Recurring revenue, LTV | 10-30% paid customers |

## 3. Eagle Camp CL5 Tối đa hoá doanh thu

5 leverage chính:
1. **Sản phẩm giá trị lớn**: tier cao cấp + giá tương xứng
2. **Bundle**: gộp nhiều sản phẩm thành package
3. **Pricing độc lập**: mỗi tier có pricing logic riêng (không % discount nhau)

## 4. Value Ladder Design Principles

### A. Bait phải có high perceived value
- Free book + shipping (Brunson Expert Secrets)
- Free webinar 3 ngày (Anna Breakout K2)
- Free audit (consulting niche)
- 199k VIP K2 = Bait paid (low risk, high commitment)

### B. Frontend phải break-even on ads
- Frontend price = CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)
- Profit at Mid + High + Continuity
- Anna Foundation 3M = break-even cho ads + lead generation

### C. Mid là profit zone
- Conversion rate Frontend → Mid = 20-40%
- AOV (Average Order Value) tier Mid quan trọng
- Anna Customer 6M = profit per customer

### D. High Ticket = 1-on-1 hoặc small group
- Personal touch (Anna direct)
- 5-25K USD typical
- Anna Coaching 50M = high ticket 1on1 6 tháng

### E. Continuity = recurring (Brunson + Hormozi đồng ý)
- Subscription monthly/quarterly
- Membership community
- Software/tool access
- Anna Cohort 1 Cohangai Q4 = continuity if monthly

## 6. Ladder Design Workflow

Step 1: Identify persona (input BC11 VPC + BC15 Character)
Step 2: Map persona income → affordable tier ceiling
Step 3: Design 3-5 tier (KHÔNG cần đầy đủ 5 tier nếu venture nhỏ)
Step 4: Each tier có name + price + deliverable + duration + guarantee
Step 5: Calculate expected conversion rate per tier
Step 6: Identify gap (where leads drop off) + add bridge tier nếu cần

## 7. Common pitfalls

❌ KHÔNG nên:
- Frontend giá quá cao (> 5M VND) → conversion drop
- Mid + High giá quá gần → no incentive upsell
- Skip Mid tier (Frontend → High direct = 1-5% conversion only)
- Continuity không có exclusive value (just lower price re-skin)
- Bait too high friction (require 10 form fields + payment)

✅ Nên:
- Bait friction thấp nhất có thể
- Frontend price = 5-10x Bait
- Mid price = 2-3x Frontend
- High price = 3-10x Mid
- Continuity = ~1/12 Mid price/tháng
