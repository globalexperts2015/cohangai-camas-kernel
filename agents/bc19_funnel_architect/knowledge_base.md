## 1. Brunson 7 Phases Funnel

### Phase 1: Pre-frame Bridge
- Mục đích: set context trước click
- Tactic: ad headline + video pre-roll + landing teaser
- Anna: FB ad headline + pre-frame video 30s

### Phase 2: Qualify Subscribers
- Mục đích: filter lead quality qua opt-in
- Tactic: lead magnet + opt-in form + ZNS/email confirmation
- Anna: Free Breakout 3 buổi tối (K2) + opt-in landing

### Phase 3: Qualify Buyers
- Mục đích: filter sẵn sàng paid qua frontend
- Tactic: low-ticket offer ($7-97) post opt-in
- Anna: VIP 199k post K2 register

### Phase 4: Identify Hyperactive Buyers
- Mục đích: upsell ngay sau frontend purchase
- Tactic: One-Time-Offer (OTO) trên thank you page
- Anna: Foundation 3M OTO post VIP

### Phase 5: Age + Ascend
- Mục đích: nurture customer leo Value Ladder
- Tactic: email sequence Day 1-30 + retargeting
- Anna: Customer 6M + Growth 15M sequence

### Phase 6: Change Selling Environment
- Mục đích: high ticket cần phone/in-person/webinar
- Tactic: Discovery call 30 phút + Coaching qualify
- Anna: Coaching 50M qualify call

### Phase 7: Identity Shift
- Mục đích: KH thành identity ("Speakout-er", "Breakouter")
- Tactic: community + ritual + member badge
- Anna: Telegram exclusive + cohort identity

## 3. Anna CIS Module 6 Phễu Sản phẩm

3 cấp phễu:
- **Đầu phễu** (low-ticket): VIP 199k + Foundation 3M
  - Cooking purpose: gateway, trust building
  - Volume: 80-100% leads
- **Giữa phễu** (mid): Customer 6M + Growth 15M
  - Purpose: deepen relationship, deliver depth
  - Volume: 10-30% paid customers
- **Cuối phễu** (high-touch): Coaching 50M
  - Purpose: transformation 1on1
  - Volume: 1-5% paid customers

Quy tắc thiết kế phễu (Anna's principles):
- Mỗi tier giải 1 pain layer sâu hơn
- Tier sau pay back tier trước
- Upsell có rationale rõ (KHÔNG manipulate)

## 7. Anti-patterns

❌ KHÔNG nên:
- Skip Pre-frame (cold ad direct to landing = waste)
- Frontend giá quá cao (>5M friction)
- KHÔNG OTO post frontend (miss 30% revenue)
- Email sequence quá ngắn (<7 emails post opt-in)
- High ticket KHÔNG có gate (lead direct purchase = no rapport)

✅ Nên:
- Pre-frame 2-3 step (ad → bridge page → landing)
- OTO immediate post payment
- 14-30 day email nurture sequence
- High ticket via discovery call gate
- Continuity ngay sau high-ticket purchase
